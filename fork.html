The Fork



Ready-made object bent fork, on common living-room color in Turkey, and IKEA frame, 23x23 cm, Istanbul, 2012


The fork belongs to the artist’s family and was used for almost 60 years. This item, a bent fork, symbolizes a kind of stasis in financial level of the artist’s family and of any other. The idea of using a bent fork also provides another possibility through insisting to use things until the end of their true lifetime, against overconsumption.
