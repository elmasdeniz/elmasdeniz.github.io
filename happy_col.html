Happy Collection


Installation,packages and waste, numbered items, dimensions variable, Istanbul, 2012


Happy Collection, 2012 is based on the value system that speculates the difference between personas of a hoarder and of an art collector. A hoarder collects invaluable objects, while the latter collects the precious ones. Hoardering, in fact suggests being free from the material-usage value of objects. Being an artist, this little collection of Denizâ€™s own trash becomes valuable in the system of art, even deserves identification through numbers that implies documentation and valuation. The hoarded objects gain a new value dimension when they are defined as an artwork.
