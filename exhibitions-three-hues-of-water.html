Three Hues of Water, Solo Exhibition

"Three Hues of Water" 14 December 2019 - 21 February 2020, Zilberman Gallery Istanbul


In her newly produced works Deniz not only critically exposes the faults of the system and its ecological problems but also points to the ways in which nature and people relate to each other. The exhibition philosophically investigates the evilness of humankind, the damage that people can cause and further points to different possibilities for a new value system. Believing that we must refocus our attention on to that which has slipped from it, she searches for a remedy for our potential extinction that will be caused by our learned defects, such as not being able to hear a seed crack or to notice the three distinct colors of a creek.

Three Hues of Water is an extension of Denizâ€™s works that were exhibited in the 16th Istanbul Biennial: The Seventh Continent.
