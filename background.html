The Background


2014, Video-slide show, Compiled photos and poetry, on LCD screen, 14’49”


In Background, 2014 we see a video in the form of a simple slide show presentation of photographs. With photographs gathered through a selection criteria of ‘people in front of beautiful scenery’ preferably in front of mountains, forests and untouched scenic beauty, the work deals with the consumption of nature as a landscape backdrop. These photographs which might as well have been shared on social media - lately as selfies, refer to our detachment of the nature. The slide show opens with the lines personifying the elements of nature: “From now on your presence will become only a background. They are not with(in) you anymore but (your) visitors.”
