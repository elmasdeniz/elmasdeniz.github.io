Lost



Dice, 31.5x32.5 cm, Istanbul, 2014


Playing with the form and its content, Lost (2014) considers ideas about uncertainty and probability with the simple item of dice. The feeling of being lost in a state of uncertainty, is written with dice offering different combinations of numbers and thus different probabilities.
