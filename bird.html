Bird ‘head’

Photo from the exhibition, ELMAS, Macka Sanat Gallery, Istanbul, Photo credit: Merve Kılıcer

Sculpture, Bird nest made by objects and tree brunches, r: 44 cm, Istanbul and Italy, 2012



Bird “Head”, 2012 is basically deals with the value system of the capitalist societies. In a symbolic manner, it proposes us to think about other possibilities than money directed, consumerist culture through use of nature as a tool for it. Is it possible to empathize with a bird or to learn from a bird? Is it possible to change our extremely logical perspective on value? We have developed a very sophisticated look to the nature. We had a certain value system that uses money to evaluate the value of things. In this way creates hierarchies of the items around us. We name things that are not related with the material but with the name of the brands.
