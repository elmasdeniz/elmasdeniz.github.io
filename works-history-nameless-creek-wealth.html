History of a particular nameless creek [Wealth]


History of a particular nameless creek [Wealth I-II], 2019, collague, 20x20 cm (each)


History of a particular nameless creek [wealth] is a collage work made of photos of tiny sculptues which excavated from Gryneion around 1800's. The use of excessive fabric on dresses on these figurines gives an idea about the class, indeed ancient remains are usually depictions of wealhy individuals.
