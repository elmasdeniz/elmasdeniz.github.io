History of a particular nameless creek [Wealth]


History of a particular nameless creek [Wealth I-II], 2019, collague, 20x20 cm (each)


Wealth I-II is a paper collage work on linen fabric. It is made of photos of tiny sculptues which excavated from Gryneion around 1800's. The use of excessive fabric on dresses on these figurines gives an idea about the class, indeed ancient remains are usually depictions of wealhy individuals. This work is from the History of a particular nameless creek series which displayed 16th Istanbul Biennial, the creek where the artist grew nearby, the work revolves around the geo-history.
